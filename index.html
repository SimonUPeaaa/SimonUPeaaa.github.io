Fortnite: From piano player to pro gamer - aged just eight

The highlight of most children's eighth birthday is blowing out the candles on their cake. However, for Joseph Deen, it was blowing the ink dry on his freshly-signed contract to be a professional game. Joseph is the youngest-ever paid Fortnite player, after signing with Team 33 in December last year The Californian esports team invited him to their headquarters to give him a $33,000 (£23,600) signing-on bonus, and high-speed computer system. Fortnite is a third-person-style shooting and building game played online by hundreds of millions of gamers around the world. "I felt amazing when I got offered the contract," Joseph told the BBC. "I've thought about being a professional gamer a lot, but no-one took me seriously until Team 33 came along."
